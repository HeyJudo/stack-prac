MOVIE REVIEW: INTERSTELLAR

Movie Description:Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet for humanity as Earth faces environmental collapse.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (9/10)
